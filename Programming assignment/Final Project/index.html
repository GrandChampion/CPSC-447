An Investigation into Software Engineering Incomes

Software engineers' salaries are hard to understand because they can vary depending on where they work, how much experience they have, and how their company pays them. There are other factors that affect salaries too, like if the engineer is a man or a woman and how expensive it is to live where they work. We made a tool to help people compare salaries across tech companies in the United States. This tool can help people understand how living expenses affect salaries and can also help them negotiate their salaries.
